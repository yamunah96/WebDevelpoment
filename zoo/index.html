Lion

  • Known as the "King of the Jungle".
  • Lions live in groups called prides.

Panda

  • Pandas eat mostly bamboo.
  • They spend up to 14 hours a day eating!

Elephant

  • Elephants are the largest land animals.
  • They have amazing memories.

Giraffe

  • They are the tallest land animals.
  • Giraffes have long purple tongues!

Tiger

  • Tigers have striped skin, not just fur.
  • They are excellent swimmers.

Kangaroo

  • They can hop up to 25 feet in a single leap.
  • Female kangaroos have a pouch for babies.

Penguin

  • Penguins cannot fly but are great swimmers.
  • They live mostly in the Southern Hemisphere.

Zebra

  • No two zebras have the same stripes.
  • Zebras can run up to 65 km/h.

Koala

  • Koalas sleep up to 20 hours a day.
  • They only eat eucalyptus leaves.

Polar Bear

  • They are excellent swimmers and can swim for days.
  • Polar bears have black skin under white fur.
