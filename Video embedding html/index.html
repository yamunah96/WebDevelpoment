World of Elephant

Elephants are gentle giants that live in deep green forests.
They walk slowly through trees and grass, making soft footsteps.
Elephants use their long trunks to pick leaves and drink cool water.
In the forest, they enjoy splashing in rivers and mud to stay cool.
Baby elephants love to play and follow their mothers everywhere.
The forest gives elephants food, water, and a safe home.
Elephants help the forest grow by spreading seeds as they walk.
They have strong family bonds and never leave each other behind.
Birds, monkeys, and many animals share the forest with them.
We must protect forests so elephants can live happy, safe lives.
